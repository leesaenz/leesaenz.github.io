Responsive Navigation

What I Hope to Learn

I hope to get better at natively impelementing responsive navigations without the use of a framework like Bootstrap or Foundation. I'd like to implement subtle animations using only CSS3 that can gracefully degrade onto devices that do not support them, which isn't much aside from old versions of IE.

How This Relates to What I've Learned Throughout My Degree

This is very important to UI design and development, because it is the main area that a user can use to find their way around your website or application. Without a good navigation, users may find themselves lost, not finding what they need from you, or going to a competitor when they give up on your service.
